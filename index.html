Welcome...

The Whakatane Surf & Lifesaving Club is based at Ohope Beach in the Bay of Plenty. In 2018 the Club celebrated its 85th anniversary and today still is an integral part of the beach and Whakatane town.

We are proud to offer our local commnity a variety of services throughout the year. We encourage all people to come and enjoy the water safely.

Read more about our programs and facilities below.

Surf School

Lessons for all!

Whakatane Surf School

From November until March we offer lessons to all who want to learn to surf. We host free open days on the first and second Saturday of November and encourage people of all abilities to join.

Lifesaving

Join our team!

Lifesaving at Whakatane

We offer lifesaving services all along the coast. We also welcome volunteer lifesavers to join our training programme and help make a difference.

New Zealand to Host New Major World Surf League Event in 2020"

Piha Pro forms part of the new WSL Challenger Series and has been confirmed as a combined men's and women's event until 2022.

AUCKLAND, New Zealand (Tuesday, September 10, 2019) - For the first time in five years, the World Surf League (WSL) will return to the pristine shores of New Zealandfor the Piha Pro, a WSL Challenger Series (CS) event on the newly-announced competition tier's calendar. The combined men's and women's events will take place in March of 2020 at the iconic Piha Beach, Auckland.
